Coco — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 June 2026

Coco (“the app”, “we”) is a mental-wellness companion that offers mood tracking, journaling, breathing and grounding tools, educational articles, peer-support circles, and an AI companion you can chat with or talk to. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is collected, how it is used, and who it is shared with. Coco is operated by Carson Rodrigues. Questions: rodriguescarson@gmail.com.

2. How we use data

  • To provide the app’s features (tracking, journaling, tools, articles, bookings, community).
  • To generate AI companion replies and transcribe voice notes you choose to record.
  • To show you your own activity and progress.
  • To keep the community safe (automated screening of posts for crisis and abusive content).

We do not sell your data, use it for third-party advertising, or share it with data brokers. We do not use tracking as defined by Apple’s App Tracking Transparency framework.

3. Third-party AI service

The AI companion and voice journaling are powered by Groq, Inc. (groq.com), a third-party AI provider in the United States. When you use these features, and only after you have given consent in the app:

  • What is sent: the messages you type to Coco (and your first name, if you set one); for voice journaling, the audio you record.
  • Why: Groq’s language model generates Coco’s reply; Groq’s speech-to-text model converts your recording into text.
  • Retention: audio is transcribed and then discarded; only the resulting text is returned to your device. We do not store your conversations or recordings on Groq.

Groq processes this data as our service provider and is contractually committed to protecting it in line with this policy. See Groq’s privacy practices at groq.com/privacy-policy. You can withdraw consent at any time in the app under You → Privacy; the AI features will then stop sending data.

4. Cloud storage and sync

If you create an account, your data is stored in Google Firebase (Authentication and Cloud Firestore) under your private user record. Without an account, your data stays on your device. See Google’s privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Not a medical service

Coco provides educational wellness content and supportive conversation. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment. It is not a substitute for professional care. Always consult a doctor or licensed clinician about medical decisions, and contact your local emergency number or a crisis line in an emergency.

6. Your choices and rights

  • Use the app without an account to keep data on-device only.
  • Withdraw AI consent at any time (You → Privacy).
  • Erase all your data from the app (You → Erase all my data).
  • Request deletion of any synced account data by emailing us.

7. Children

Coco is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes

We may update this policy; we will revise the “Last updated” date above. Material changes to AI data handling will re-prompt you for consent in the app.
